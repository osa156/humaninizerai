Welcome to Grace Haven

Where Faith Finds Rest & Souls Find Refuge

Our Sacred Mission

Grace Haven Church & Retreat Center stands as a beacon of hope and tranquility, nestled in the heart of nature's embrace. For over three decades, we have served as a spiritual sanctuary where believers gather to worship, reflect, and renew their faith.

Our unique ministry combines traditional worship with modern hospitality, offering not just spiritual nourishment but also peaceful accommodation for those seeking respite from the world's chaos. Whether you're here for Sunday service or a week-long retreat, you'll find a home among our community.

Our History & Heritage

A legacy of faith spanning generations

1989

Foundation Stone Laid

Founded by Pastor John Mitchell with a vision to create a place where urban weary souls could find rural peace. The original chapel seated just 50 people.

1995

First Retreat Center

Added our first 10 guest rooms to accommodate visiting missionaries and retreat participants. The beginning of our hospitality ministry.

2008

New Sanctuary Built

Completed our current sanctuary with stunning stained glass windows and state-of-the-art audio-visual systems while maintaining traditional architecture.

2024

Digital Expansion

Launched our virtual tour and online booking system, allowing guests worldwide to experience Grace Haven before they even arrive.
